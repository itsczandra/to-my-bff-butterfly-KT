To my Kambal Tuko a.k.a Rio Jirah, Acabal-Ito,

July 22, 2026.

Honestly, when I saw these butterfly earrings, ikaw agad ang naisip ko.

I don't know if I've ever told you this, but I've always seen you as a butterfly. Not because you're soft or delicate, but because I've watched you grow into the amazing woman you are today.

We've been best friends since kindergarten—almost our whole lives. For more than two decades, we've seen each other through awkward phases, big dreams, heartbreaks, achievements, happy tears, and everything in between. We've literally grown up together, and I honestly can't imagine my life without you in it.

I've seen you transform so many times, and every version of you has been beautiful in its own way. Just like a butterfly, you've always found a way to grow, even when life wasn't easy.

🦋 Twenty-four years. Countless memories. One forever friendship. 🦋

Now you're about to start the most beautiful chapter yet—becoming a wife. And I couldn't be happier because I know you found someone who loves you for exactly who you are.

I hope these butterfly earrings become more than just jewelry. I hope they remind you of how far you've come, how strong you've always been, and that even as life changes, you'll continue to grow into even more beautiful versions of yourself.

Thank you for being my constant for all these years. Thank you for every laugh, every secret, every adventure, every random conversation, and for always being the kind of friend who feels like family.

No matter where life takes us, no matter how busy we become, you'll always be my best friend. That will never change.

I'm so proud of the woman you've become, and I'm so excited to watch you build this new life with the person you love.

Congratulations on your wedding. May your marriage be full of love, patience, laughter, and countless beautiful memories.

I love you so much, always.

Love,
Your KT ❤️
Cazandra Barrameda

Wherever life takes you... whether you're just across the city or already building your new home in Japan, you'll always have someone here cheering for you.
